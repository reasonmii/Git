Open gitKraken
Click 'Open a repo' - 'Open a Repository' - Click your folder ex) 'myFirstGit'
Now we can see our commit history of the repository

When you change or edit something, 
you can see on the right side, saying "7 files changes on master"
- pencil icon : edited
- - icon : deleted
- + icon : created
